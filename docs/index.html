Practice that actually sticks

Pick your chords, set a delay, and hit play. LoopChords shows one chord big in the center with its fretboard, previews the next one in the corner, and keeps looping — training the muscle memory you need for smooth, fast chord changes.

  • Random order — no memorizing a fixed sequence
  • Adjustable 1–5 second delay
  • Next-chord preview so you're always ready
  • Live elapsed-time counter, pause & skip
